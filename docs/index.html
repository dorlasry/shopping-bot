Privacy Policy — Shopping List Bot

Last updated: May 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Shopping List Bot ("the Service", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with it on WhatsApp. The Service is a personal project that lets families and couples manage a shared shopping list by sending messages on WhatsApp.

Information we collect

When you message the Service, we process:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number — to identify you and your shared list.
  • Your WhatsApp display name — to personalize replies.
  • Message content — the text you send (e.g. shopping items) so we can add, remove, or display items.
  • Timestamps — when items are added or marked as bought.

We do not collect payment information, location, or your contacts.

How we use your information

We use this information solely to operate the shopping-list service: to understand your requests, maintain your shared list, and reply to you on WhatsApp. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell it.

Third-party services

To provide the Service we share the minimum necessary data with Meta / WhatsApp (to send and receive messages) and Anthropic (Claude API) (to interpret your message text and extract item names). These providers process data only to deliver the Service.

Data retention

We retain your list data until you ask us to delete it or stop using the Service. You can request deletion at any time using the contact below.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. You can also stop using the Service at any time by no longer messaging the bot.

Children's privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction) and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest revision.
