# Privacy Policy — Shopping List Bot

_Last updated: May 24, 2026_

This Privacy Policy explains how the **Shopping List Bot** ("the Service", "we",
"us") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with it on
WhatsApp. The Service is a personal project that lets families and couples manage
a shared shopping list by sending messages on WhatsApp.

## Information we collect

When you message the Service, we process:

- **Your WhatsApp phone number** — used to identify you and your shared list.
- **Your WhatsApp display name** — used to personalize replies.
- **Message content** — the text you send (e.g. shopping items) so we can add,
  remove, or display items on your list.
- **Timestamps** — when items are added or marked as bought.

We do **not** collect payment information, location, contacts, or any data
beyond what is described above.

## How we use your information

We use this information solely to operate the shopping-list service: to
understand your requests, maintain your shared list, and reply to you on
WhatsApp. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell it.

## Third-party services

To provide the Service we share the minimum necessary data with:

- **Meta / WhatsApp** — to send and receive messages on the WhatsApp Business
  Platform (governed by Meta's own privacy policy).
- **Anthropic (Claude API)** — message text is sent to Anthropic's API to
  interpret your request (e.g. extract item names). See Anthropic's privacy
  policy for how they handle data.

These providers process data only to deliver the Service.

## Data retention

We retain your list data until you ask us to delete it or stop using the
Service. You can request deletion of your data at any time using the contact
details below, and we will remove your stored information.

## Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by
contacting us. You can also stop using the Service at any time by no longer
messaging the bot.

## Children's privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your
jurisdiction) and we do not knowingly collect their data.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at
the top reflects the latest revision.
